Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Xiaofang Yang, founder of Qentro.

Over the past 20+ years, I've worked across product engineering, infrastructure, operations, and digital transformation, helping organizations deliver complex technology initiatives and solve business challenges. Along the way, I learned that successful technology isn't about having the newest tools - it's about solving the right problems.

That belief continues to guide everything we do at Qentro.

How Qentro Began

Respect and Protect.

Qentro didn't start as an AI company.

It started with a simple idea: Respect and Protect - respect the experience and knowledge of those who came before us, and protect the opportunities of those who will shape the future.

My original vision was different, but conversations with customers taught me an important lesson: passion gives you purpose, but customers shape the business. As organizations began looking for practical ways to adopt AI while maintaining control of their data, Qentro evolved into the company it is today.

Although the business evolved, the values behind Qentro have remained the same.

Looking Forward
