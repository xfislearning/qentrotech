Community Initiative

Qentro Community Apprenticeship Program

A separate community-focused volunteer initiative connecting experienced professionals with new graduates and career learners through practical guidance and career support.

Volunteer as a Mentor Sign Up as a New Graduate

Purpose

Respecting experience. Supporting future generations.

The Apprenticeship Program is a community-focused volunteer initiative. It operates independently from Qentro's commercial AI services and is not a paid consulting offering.

Its purpose is simple: help new graduates gain real-world guidance while giving experienced professionals a meaningful way to share their knowledge.

Who It Serves

Not an AI service. A volunteer community program.

Volunteer Mentors

Retired, semi-retired, or experienced professionals who are willing to share career advice, practical knowledge, project feedback, and encouragement.

New Graduates

Students and early-career professionals looking for guidance, confidence, workplace understanding, and exposure to real-world professional expectations.

How It Works

Simple, practical, and volunteer-based.

01

Sign Up

Mentors and graduates submit their background, interests, and availability.

02

Review

We review profiles to understand goals, experience, and possible fit.

03

Match

When there is a good fit, we connect mentors and graduates for conversations or project guidance.

04

Support

Participants learn, share, and grow through mentorship and practical career support.
