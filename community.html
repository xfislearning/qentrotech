The Qentro Apprenticeship Program.

A volunteer initiative connecting experienced professionals with new graduates and career learners through practical guidance and career support. It operates independently from Qentro's commercial AI services and is not a paid offering.

Volunteer Mentors

Share what you know.

New Graduates

Grow with guidance.

Students and early-career professionals looking for guidance, confidence, workplace understanding, and exposure to real-world professional expectations.

How it works: sign up with your background and interests. We review profiles for fit. When there's a good match, we connect mentors and graduates for conversations or project guidance. Matches depend on mentor availability and fit.
