The Qentro Privacy Levels

Choose exactly where your data lives.

Every solution is built to one of three named privacy levels. This is agreed in writing before any work starts.

Level 3 — Fully Local

Nothing leaves your building.

AI runs on hardware you own. No cloud, no external APIs, and no token costs. For regulated data and maximum-sensitivity work.

Level 2 — Private Cloud

Your dedicated environment.

AI runs in an isolated cloud environment under your control. No data used for model training, ever.

Level 1 — Hybrid

Sensitive stays local, the rest scales.

Sensitive data is processed locally; non-sensitive workloads use vetted cloud AI for speed and cost.
